Purpose Protected Me

Pastor Rohan Samuels

December 7, 2025 10 min read
Purpose Protection Divine Providence
Watch Sermon Download PDF

Experience the complete message as Pastor Rohan Samuels explores how God's purpose provides divine protection in our lives.

Main Idea

When we walk in God's purpose, we are under His divine protection. Purpose becomes our shield against the attacks of the enemy, and God's plans for our lives cannot be thwarted by opposition. Understanding and embracing your purpose is not just about fulfillment—it's about positioning yourself under the covering of God's providential care.

Key Scriptures

Jeremiah 29:11

"For I know the plans I have for you," declares the LORD, "plans to prosper you and not to harm you, plans to give you hope and a future."

Romans 8:28

"And we know that in all things God works for the good of those who love him, who have been called according to his purpose."

Psalm 138:8

"The LORD will vindicate me; your love, LORD, endures forever—do not abandon the works of your hands."

Sermon Outline

1. Purpose Provides Direction

  • When you know your purpose, you can't be easily distracted
  • God's purpose acts as a compass through life's uncertainties

2. Purpose Positions You for Protection

  • Living in purpose places you under God's covering
  • What the enemy means for harm, God uses for His glory
  • Your purpose is your shield against attacks

Personal Application Notes:

3. Purpose Propels You Forward

  • God will complete what He started in you
  • Opposition confirms you're on the right path

Personal Application Notes:

Discussion Questions

  1. 1. How have you experienced God's protection as you've walked in your purpose?
  2. 2. What challenges are you currently facing that might actually be confirmation you're moving in the right direction?
  3. 3. How can understanding your God-given purpose help you navigate seasons of uncertainty?
  4. 4. In what ways have you seen God work all things together for good in your life, even difficult circumstances?
  5. 5. What steps can you take this week to more fully embrace and walk in your divine purpose?

Group Discussion Notes:

Prayer Points

  • Pray for clarity in understanding God's purpose for your life
  • Ask God for courage to walk boldly in your calling despite opposition
  • Thank God for His protection as you pursue His plans
  • Pray for those who feel purposeless or discouraged
  • Ask God to complete the good work He has begun in you

Personal Prayer Requests:

This Week's Challenge

Take time this week to write down what you believe God's purpose is for your life. Identify one area where you've been holding back because of fear or opposition, and take one bold step forward in faith.

Remember: Your purpose protects you. Walk boldly in what God has called you to do.
