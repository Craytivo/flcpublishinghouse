About FLC Publishing House

FLC Publishing House is a ministry of Freedom Life Church, dedicated to equipping believers and leaders with premium sermon notes, Bible studies, and devotionals. Our resources are crafted to help you engage deeply with Scripture, grow in faith, and lead others with confidence.

“Our mission is to make the Word of God accessible, practical, and transformative for every believer.”

Whether you’re a pastor, small group leader, or a passionate learner, you’ll find tools here to enrich your spiritual journey and teaching ministry. We believe in the power of well-prepared resources to inspire, instruct, and ignite faith in every season of life.

Explore our library for sermon notes, devotionals, and downloadable resources—each designed to help you grow and lead with confidence.

What Makes Us Unique?

  • Original, in-depth sermon notes and studies
  • Devotionals for every season and age group
  • Practical resources for leaders and families
  • Rooted in Scripture, relevant for today
